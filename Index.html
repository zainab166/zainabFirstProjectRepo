My Zainab

Your eyes ignite constellations in my twilight soul,

In the cathedral of your heart, I find my sacred whole.

Your touch - a calm written in summer's golden fire,

Our breaths entwine like vines of endless desire.

Zainab, my compass in life's tempestuous sea,

In the algebra of love, you're my eternity.

Let our story blaze across heaven's infinite night,

Two souls fused as one in supernova's light.
